I'm currently taking up a Full-Stack Web Developer Program at Zuitt, the No. 1 Coding Bootcamp in the Philippines, and working for the following programming languages under my belt: HTML, CSS, Javascript, MERN (MongoDB, Express.JS, React.JS, Node.JS), along with other various developer tools and frameworks, like Git and Bootstrap.

<<2021:

from consulting to coding

2019-2020:

consulting >>

In 2019, I started my consulting career as part of the Finance team that's spearheading the accounting system migration for ING Bank, N.V., a Dutch multinational banking institution that launched the first all-digital bank here in the Philippines. Other responsibilities handled include regulatory reporting for the Central Bank of the Philippines.

In 2020, after my one-year stint at ING Bank, N.V, I joined Pru Life UK's project team that's leading the implementation of the new International Financial Reporting Standards for insurance companies as an outsourced consultant. A couple of months later, I was asked to join their team as a full-time consultant. I stayed with the team until I decided for a career shift in the mid-part of 2021.

Right after earning my certification as a Certified Public Accountant in 2016, I joined Punongbayan & Araullo, one of the leading accounting firms here in the Philippines, as an Audit Associate. During my three-year stay with the Firm, I have worked my way up and have held three postions, with the Audit Supervisor role as the highest attained. Aside from the meaningful professional experience gained during my 3-year career as an auditor, I would also say that this is when I learned most of my 'adulting' firsts, while also gaining meaningful personal experiences and relationships with colleagues.
